## Exercise Bike Settings

```
Duration:     15 minutes per session
Resistance:   Moderate (3-5 out of 10)
Cadence:      60-80 RPM (steady, not sprinting)
Heart Rate:   120-130 bpm (conversational pace)
Calories:     ~150-200 per session

Execution:
- AM: First thing after waking OR before leaving for gym
- PM: After work, after dinner, or before bed
- Watch a show, YouTube, or listen to podcast
- Keep it easy - you should be able to breathe through your nose
- If HR exceeds 135, drop resistance
- Consistency > intensity
```

## Troubleshooting

### "I don't have time for 30 min"
- Wake up 15 min earlier
- Do PM session during commercial breaks
- Split it: 10 min + 5 min if needed

### "The bike is boring"
- Watch YouTube/Netflix
- Listen to podcasts/audiobooks
- Put the bike in front of a TV

### "My knees hurt"
- Lower the resistance
- Try the treadmill (walking) instead
- Check bike seat height

### "I missed a session"
- Don't try to "make up" with 30 min next time
- Just do the next scheduled 15 min session
- Consistency > perfection
